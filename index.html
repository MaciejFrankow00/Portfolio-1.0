A simple 3D platformer created in Unreal Engine 5.4.4, where the player explores a level, collects coins, and overcomes terrain obstacles. Unity Action RPG Combat System Skill Tree Isometric
Hack and Slash is an isometric action game where the player takes on the role of a hero traversing a diverse world and facing waves of enemies in a fast, dynamic combat style. Gameplay relies on a fluid control system; WSAD handles character movement, while Spacebar performs a quick dodge to evade enemy attacks. The player also has access to a skill tree menu via the 'C' key, where they can develop the character by investing in selected attributes and abilities. The main goal of the game is to complete a story quest involving the elimination of two key NPCs, which constitutes the first major challenge in the game world. The combat and character development system combines classic RPG elements with dynamic action. The player gains experience levels, allowing them to unlock or upgrade combat abilities such as: Key Mechanics and Systems: - Dynamic Combat System – fluid movement (WSAD) combined with a quick dodge mechanic (Spacebar), allowing for tactical evasion of attacks. - Special Abilities – the player has an array of skills: Shockwave (AOE impact), Fireball (exploding projectile), and Buff (temporary damage reduction). - Skill Tree (Menu 'C') – a system for investing in four main attributes: Strength (physical damage), Dexterity (attack/dodge speed), Health (max HP), and Wisdom (mana regen and magic power). - Artificial Intelligence – enemies patrol the area and react to the player, forcing active use of defensive mechanics. - Save System – a mechanism to save progress and character development, allowing the player to return to the game at any time. - Fireball – a ball of fire that ignites hit enemies and explodes on impact. - Shockwave (AOE) – a shockwave radiating from the hero, damaging all enemies in range. - Buff (damage reduction) – a defensive skill that temporarily reduces incoming damage. Character development takes place through a skill tree system covering four main attributes: - Strength – increases physical damage. - Dexterity – affects attack and dodge speed. - Health – increases maximum health points. - Wisdom – strengthens magic abilities and mana regeneration. The game features a progress saving system that preserves character development and acquired skills, allowing gameplay to continue at any moment. Enemies in the game are equipped with a simple AI system; they can patrol terrain and perform basic attacks, which, combined with dodge mechanics and abilities, creates dynamic and tactical encounters.
